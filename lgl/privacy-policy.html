Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

This Privacy Policy explains how Vitalix LLC ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use this Website and purchase Mediterra products.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (such as name, shipping and billing address, email, and phone number), payment information processed by our payment providers, and information collected automatically (such as device, browser, and usage data through cookies and similar technologies).

2. How We Use Your Information

3. Sharing of Information

We share information with service providers who help us operate our business, including fulfillment partners (such as ShipOffers), payment processors, and analytics providers. We do not sell your personal information.

We and our third-party payment service providers may receive updated card information from your card issuer (for example, updated card numbers or expiration dates). You may opt out of your card's updating service by contacting your card issuer.

5. Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at support@usemediterra.com. You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.

We implement reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
