Returns Policy

Our 30-day money-back guarantee and refund process.

Mediterra offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on eligible orders. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll make it right.

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

You may request a refund within thirty (30) days from the date you received your order. To start a return or refund, email support@usemediterra.com with your order number. Our team will respond within 1–2 business days to confirm next steps.

2. How Refunds Are Issued

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Depending on your bank, it may take several business days for the refund to appear.

3. Subscriptions

Cancelling a subscription stops all future recurring charges. Charges already processed are handled under this Returns Policy. You can cancel any time on our cancellation page.

4. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact us at support@usemediterra.com and we will arrange a replacement or refund.
